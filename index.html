Welcome to OpenAddresses

We're collecting every single address in the world, associating it with a point on Earth, and letting you download the whole thing as a CSV.

Why do this?

Addresses are a helpful dataset

Where can I get data?

Right now we're collecting and collating data sources. We don't have a compiled dataset yet, but you can check out our list of data sources and add your own.

How can I help?

You can help immediately by filling out this form.

If you're comfortable with GitHub, you can find your local data set and open an issue with information about how to access it and what area is covered.
